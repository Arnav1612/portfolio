Who I Am & What I Do

I'm Arnav Singh, a software developer & AI trainer based in Ghaziabad, UP, India. I graduated with a B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering from Manipal University Jaipur, with a deep focus on backend systems, data engineering, and machine learning.

My most recent and ongoing work is as a Freelance AI Trainer & Data Annotator on platforms like Outlier AI and Scale AI — I write and evaluate code, math & reasoning prompts, run RLHF feedback cycles, and red-team LLMs to improve alignment and accuracy. This work sits at the intersection of software engineering and AI research.

Alongside this, I have hands-on industry experience building REST APIs, backend systems, and data pipelines at Learnoprod Technologies and IDEAL TECHS, working with Node.js, Python, MongoDB, and Java in production environments.
